How It Works

For Donors

  1. Register as a Food Donor
  2. Post available surplus food
  3. Receive requests from receivers
  4. Approve and arrange pickup
  5. Reduce food waste!

For Receivers

  1. Register as NGO/Volunteer/Organization
  2. Browse available food
  3. Request food you need
  4. Arrange pickup time
  5. Help people in need!
