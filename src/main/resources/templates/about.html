About FoodShare

Our Mission

To reduce food waste and combat hunger by connecting food donors with organizations and individuals in need.

The Problem

Every day, millions of tons of edible food are wasted while millions of people go hungry. There's a disconnect between food donors (restaurants, hotels, bakeries, supermarkets) and receivers (NGOs, charities, volunteers, orphanages).

Our Solution

FoodShare is a digital platform that bridges this gap, enabling:

  • Easy listing and discovery of surplus food
  • Quick request and approval process
  • Real-time communication between donors and receivers
  • Tracking of collected food and impact statistics

Our Impact

  • Reduced food waste
  • Improved food security
  • Strengthened communities
  • Environmental benefits
